DAILY

Daily is a modern, minimalistic productivity tool designed as a UI/UX thought experiment by Daniel King. Initially frustrated by the planners/to-do list applications currently on the market with frustrating UI's and unintuitive layouts, Daniel decided to create his own application - one designed with simplicity and funcitonality in mind.
