about.

Hi. I'm Daniel - a 19 year old second year computer science student at Western Washington University fascinated with the role of digital media in the human experience. I enjoy creating functional and elegant applications with an emphasis on creative and unique UI/UX. I like to implement different design philosophies and experiment with visual elements in order to enhance the interactivity and user experience of the product.

Lately, I've been pretty interested in machine learning, data science, and data visualization. When i'm not busy with coursework or playing around with various side projects, I enjoy writing, drawing, and cooking.
